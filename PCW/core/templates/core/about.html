About Us

Welcome to Puddle — your smart companion for saving money on groceries.

In today’s world, prices can change quickly and vary widely from store to store. We created this platform to help everyday shoppers make informed choices by finding the cheapest grocery prices in their area, all in one place.

Why We Exist

Let’s face it — no one wants to overpay for essentials. Whether you're buying fresh produce, pantry staples, or your favorite snacks, we believe you should know where to get the best deal. Our mission is to simplify grocery shopping by showing you real-time pricing across local supermarkets and stores near you.

What We Offer

  • Price Comparison: Search and compare prices for thousands of grocery items across nearby stores.
  • Location-Based Results: Get results based on your location for convenience and accuracy.
  • Smart Shopping: Plan your trips and stretch your budget with better decision-making.
  • Transparency: No gimmicks, no hidden markups — just honest data to help you save.

Our Vision

We’re building a future where grocery shopping is as smart as it is simple — where you can make better choices without wasting time hopping between stores or websites.

Let’s Save Together

We’re more than just a price comparison tool. We're a community of smart shoppers who believe that saving money should be easy, accessible, and empowering.
